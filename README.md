# Matamáticas

Matamáticas is a fun and fast-paced browser game designed to test your multiplication skills. 
In this game, you control a crosshair to shoot down falling tanks, each displaying a number.
Your goal is to shoot the tanks that correctly answer the multiplication problem shown at the bottom of the screen.

## How to Play

1.  **Start the Game**: Open the `index.html` file in your web browser.
2.  **Choose a Difficulty**: Select a difficulty level to determine the range of numbers in the multiplication problems.
3.  **Aim and Shoot**:
    *   Move your mouse to aim the crosshair.
    *   Click the left mouse button or press the `Shift` key to shoot.
4.  **Objective**:
    *   An operation (e.g., `5 × 8`) will be displayed at the bottom of the screen.
    *   Shoot the falling tank that displays the correct answer (in this case, `40`).
    *   Shooting a tank with the correct answer increases your score.
    *   Shooting an incorrect tank or letting a correct tank fall off-screen will result in a penalty.
5.  **Bonus Tanks**: Occasionally, special bonus tanks will appear. Shooting these will grant you extra bullets.
